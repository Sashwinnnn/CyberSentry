Stay Safe Online

A Friendly Guide to Protecting Yourself

Welcome! We are going to learn about the four main things that keep you safe: Passwords, Phishing, Malware, and Keyloggers.

🔒 Passwords: Your Front Door Lock

❌ Weak Password

Short, one word, or a name.
Example: fluffy1

✅ Strong Passphrase

3-4 random words combined.
Example: BlueSkyHappyFish

Quick Tip: Use a different passphrase for your bank than you use for your everyday email.

🎣 Phishing: Fake Bait

⚠️ Scam Example Email:

From: alerts@secure-bank-verify.com

"Your account is SUSPENDED! Click HERE now!"

  • Creates Panic: "Act NOW or lose everything!"
  • Strange Sender: Not from your real bank's true address.
  • Asks for Clicks: Real banks don't email you links to sign in.

🦠 Malware: Bad Software

Malware = "Malicious Software". Programs designed to spy or steal.

How it gets in:

• Suspicious email links
• "Free" unknown programs

How to stay safe:

• Keep computer updated
• Use antivirus software

⌨️ Keyloggers: The Tape Recorder

Imagine a tiny tape recorder hidden inside your keyboard—recording every single letter you type.

Level Up Your Safety

Essential Survival Guide for the Internet

Whether you're on Roblox, Discord, or YouTube, staying safe is the only way to protect your account and your progress.

🔐 Pro Password Strategy

Hackers use scripts that guess thousands of passwords a second. Don't make it easy.

❌ NO NO's

Using your pet's name, your birthday, or "12345".

✅ PRO TIP

Use random words: PizzaDragonSkate88

Sentry Rule: Never share your password with friends online, even if they promise you free items.

🎣 The "Free Currency" Scam

⚠️ Watch Out For:

"Click here for 10,000 FREE ROBUX / V-BUCKS! Just log in to claim!"

  • The Trick: The link takes you to a fake login page to steal your account.
  • The Truth: No one gives away money for free online. If it's too good to be true, it's a scam.

🦠 The "Free Mod" Trap

Malware often hides inside "free hacks" or "game mods" found on YouTube or Discord.

The Risk:

Sketchy files can steal every password saved in your browser and use your computer to send spam.

Stay Safe:

Don't download files from strangers. Ask a parent before installing new software.

⌨️ Keyboard Watchers

Keyloggers are a gamer's worst nightmare. They watch every key you press.
